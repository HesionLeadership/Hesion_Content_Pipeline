## Summary
Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), India's largest IT services company, has reorganized its leadership team and created new business units, signaling a strategic restructuring of its organizational architecture.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Leadership team restructuring and the creation of new business units reflect strategic organizational design decisions that can impact role clarity, accountability, and cross-functional coordination. Such reorganizations often serve as signals of strategic pivots and can significantly affect employee motivation and alignment depending on how the transition is managed.

## Leadership Lesson
When restructuring leadership teams and creating new business units, CHROs and COOs should proactively manage role ambiguity and change fatigue by clearly communicating the strategic rationale, ensuring defined accountability structures, and supporting leaders transitioning into newly created roles to maintain organizational momentum.
