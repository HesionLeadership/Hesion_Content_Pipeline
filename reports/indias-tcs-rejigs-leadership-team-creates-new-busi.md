## Summary
Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), India's largest IT services company, has restructured its leadership team and created new business units, signaling a strategic organizational redesign under its leadership.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Large-scale organizational restructuring involving leadership team reshuffling and the creation of new business units reflects strategic realignment efforts, which research shows can significantly impact employee morale, role clarity, and organizational identity if not managed with transparent communication and change management practices.

## Leadership Lesson
When restructuring leadership and creating new business units, CHROs and COOs should prioritize clear communication of strategic rationale, ensure role clarity for newly appointed leaders, and invest in change management processes to minimize disruption and maintain organizational cohesion during transitions.
