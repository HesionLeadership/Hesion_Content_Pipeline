## Summary
Walmart announced a significant initiative to promote from within, highlighting internal mobility and career advancement opportunities for its best-performing employees.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Internal promotion practices are strongly linked to employee motivation, organizational commitment, and retention. Signaling a promote-from-within culture activates perceived organizational support and enhances psychological contract fulfillment among high performers.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should formalize and visibly communicate internal promotion pathways—doing so strengthens retention of top talent, reduces recruitment costs, and signals that employee development is a genuine strategic priority rather than mere rhetoric.
