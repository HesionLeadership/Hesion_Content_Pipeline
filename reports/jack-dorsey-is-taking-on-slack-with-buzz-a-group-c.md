## Summary
Jack Dorsey is launching Buzz, a workplace group chat platform designed to integrate human team members and AI agents into the same conversations, positioning it as a competitor to Slack.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Integrating AI agents as quasi-team members in group communication channels fundamentally reshapes team dynamics, communication norms, and potentially the psychological experience of collaboration—raising questions about trust, social presence, and how humans relate to non-human 'teammates.'

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should proactively consider how introducing AI agents into team communication platforms will affect team cohesion, psychological safety, and communication patterns. Designing clear norms around human-AI interaction in teams is essential before deploying these tools at scale.
