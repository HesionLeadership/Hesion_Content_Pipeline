# Synthesia’s AI training platform is moving beyond videos into live coaching

**Source:** TechCrunch  
**Published:** Wed, 22 Jul 2026 08:00:00 +0000  
**URL:** [https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/22/synthesias-ai-training-platform-is-moving-beyond-videos-into-live-coaching/](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/22/synthesias-ai-training-platform-is-moving-beyond-videos-into-live-coaching/)

## Summary
Synthesia launched AI Roleplay Sessions, an interactive training platform where employees practice workplace conversations with AI avatars that provide real-time feedback, scoring, and analytics to help enterprises measure training effectiveness.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
AI-driven role-play addresses a longstanding gap in behavioral skill transfer—moving from passive video-based learning to active practice with immediate feedback, which aligns with deliberate practice theory and enhances self-efficacy in interpersonal skills.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs should evaluate whether AI simulation tools can supplement or replace traditional role-play exercises for high-stakes conversations (e.g., difficult feedback, sales objections), but must ensure psychological safety so employees view AI coaching as developmental rather than surveillance-based performance monitoring.
