## Summary
Honest Company CEO Carla Vernón discusses the decision-making, communication, and focus required to lead a mission-driven company through turbulent times and toward growth.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Leading a mission-driven organization during uncertainty requires disciplined strategic focus and clear communication to align stakeholders around purpose while navigating competing pressures. This touches on how leaders use organizational identity and mission clarity as anchoring mechanisms during volatility.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should ensure that in turbulent environments, leadership communication consistently reinforces strategic priorities and mission alignment—excessive pivoting or ambiguity erodes trust and dilutes organizational focus. Narrowing decision-making criteria to what serves the core mission helps teams stay productive under uncertainty.
