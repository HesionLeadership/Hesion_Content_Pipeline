## Summary
Ukraine's President Zelenskyy is considering firing Armed Forces commander-in-chief Syrskyi following days of internal military turmoil and public protests, signaling a major leadership shakeup during wartime.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates the tension between maintaining organizational stability and responding to external pressure (public protests) when making senior leadership dismissal decisions. It raises questions about whether leadership changes driven by external legitimacy pressures versus performance metrics lead to better or worse organizational outcomes.

## Leadership Lesson
When facing public pressure to remove a senior leader, executives must distinguish between genuine performance failures and scapegoating dynamics. CHROs should ensure succession planning is robust enough that leadership transitions under crisis conditions don't destabilize the organization further.
