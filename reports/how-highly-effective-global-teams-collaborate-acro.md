## Organizational Psychology Angle
Cross-cultural team effectiveness depends on structured collaboration frameworks that address communication norms, trust-building, and shared mental models—all well-established constructs in organizational psychology research on multinational teams.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should invest in explicit team operating agreements that address cultural communication styles, decision-making protocols, and asynchronous collaboration norms rather than assuming teams will organically adapt to cross-cultural differences.
