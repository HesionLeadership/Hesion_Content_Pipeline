## Summary
Marco Bassetti discusses how global entertainment companies must adapt to local cultures rather than imposing a one-size-fits-all approach, emphasizing the importance of cultural sensitivity in content strategy.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects the tension between global standardization and local adaptation in multinational organizations—a core challenge in cross-cultural management. Leaders must balance organizational coherence with the autonomy needed for local teams to create culturally resonant products.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs leading global operations should resist the temptation to over-centralize strategy and instead empower local leaders with decision-making authority, recognizing that cultural intelligence at the local level drives market relevance and employee engagement.
