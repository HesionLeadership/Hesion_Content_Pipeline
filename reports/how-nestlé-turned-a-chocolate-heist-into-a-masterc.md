## Summary
When a truckload of KitKats was stolen, Nestlé's marketing team turned the crisis into a brand-building opportunity by using humor in their response, with their European marketing head explaining the strategy behind the decision.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates how organizational agility and psychological safety within a marketing team enabled a rapid, unconventional crisis response—teams that feel empowered to take creative risks can turn threats into opportunities.

## Leadership Lesson
Leaders should cultivate decision-making cultures where teams have the autonomy and psychological safety to respond quickly and creatively to unexpected events, rather than defaulting to rigid, risk-averse protocols. Empowering middle management to act boldly in ambiguous situations can transform crises into competitive advantages.
