# The Best Leaders Aren’t Replacing Employees With AI, They’re Empowering Them

**Source:** Inc  
**Published:** Mon, 13 Jul 2026 10:00:00 -0400

## Summary
The article argues that the most effective leaders are using AI to augment and empower their employees rather than replacing them, positioning human-AI collaboration as the future of work.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects the job demands-resources (JD-R) model and augmentation theory—when leaders frame AI as a resource that enhances employee capability rather than a threat, it preserves psychological safety, increases engagement, and reduces technostress. Empowerment-focused leadership during technological disruption aligns with research on empowering leadership and technology acceptance.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should design AI adoption strategies that explicitly position technology as a tool to enhance employee roles rather than eliminate them. Communicate a clear human-centered AI vision, invest in upskilling, and involve employees in AI implementation decisions to maintain trust and engagement during digital transformation.
