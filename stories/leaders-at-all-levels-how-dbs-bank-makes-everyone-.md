# Leaders at All Levels: How DBS Bank Makes Everyone an Innovator

## Summary
DBS Bank has embedded innovation into its performance management system by making it a KPI worth 20% of every team and individual's performance review, aiming to make innovation everyone's responsibility rather than a top-down mandate.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Tying innovation to formal performance metrics across all organizational levels represents a structural approach to democratizing creative behavior, leveraging goal-setting theory and extrinsic motivation to shift cultural norms around who is responsible for innovation.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should consider how performance management systems can be redesigned to signal strategic priorities—embedding innovation (or any critical behavior) as a measurable KPI across all levels can shift it from aspirational rhetoric to accountable practice, though leaders must balance extrinsic incentives with intrinsic motivation to avoid undermining creativity.
