## Summary
The article argues that the most effective leaders are using AI to augment and empower their employees rather than replacing them, positioning human-AI collaboration as the future of work.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This aligns with research on job enrichment and empowerment-focused leadership, where providing employees with better tools and autonomy increases engagement, performance, and retention. The framing reflects the tension between technological determinism and human-centered organizational design.

## Leadership Lesson
CHROs and COOs should frame AI adoption as an employee empowerment initiative rather than a cost-cutting measure—investing in training, redesigning roles around augmented capabilities, and communicating that technology serves people, not the reverse. This approach reduces resistance to change and preserves psychological safety during transformation.
