## Summary
Berkshire Hathaway has accumulated $397 billion in cash reserves, interpreted as Warren Buffett's strategic bet against an overheated market by staying on the sidelines rather than deploying capital.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects Buffett's contrarian leadership philosophy and organizational decision-making discipline—the willingness to resist herd mentality and maintain strategic patience despite external pressure to deploy capital, which is a hallmark of high-conviction leadership.
