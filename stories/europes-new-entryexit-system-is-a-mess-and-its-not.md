# Europe’s New Entry/Exit System Is a Mess, and It’s Not Going Away

## Summary
The EU's new biometric Entry/Exit System has caused severe delays at airports, and EU leaders have refused the aviation industry's request to pause the rollout despite operational chaos and missed flights.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates a classic large-scale organizational implementation failure: leaders pushing forward with a poorly executed system rollout despite clear operational evidence of dysfunction, reflecting sunk-cost commitment escalation and a disconnect between policy decision-makers and frontline operational realities.
