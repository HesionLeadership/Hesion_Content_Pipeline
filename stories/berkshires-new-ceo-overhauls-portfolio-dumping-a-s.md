# Berkshire's new CEO overhauls portfolio, dumping a slate of stocks

**Source:** CNBC CEO  
**Published:** Sat, 16 May 2026 13:18:09 GMT  
**URL:** [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/berkshires-new-ceo-overhauls-portfolio-dumping-a-slate-of-stocks.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/berkshires-new-ceo-overhauls-portfolio-dumping-a-slate-of-stocks.html)

## Summary
Greg Abel, Berkshire Hathaway's new CEO, has made one of the biggest overhauls to the company's equity portfolio in his first three months, dumping a slate of stocks.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates a classic leadership succession dynamic: a new CEO asserting strategic identity and breaking from a legendary predecessor's legacy through bold, visible action to establish authority and signal a new direction.

## Strength Score
**7/10** — The story directly touches on leadership succession, the psychology of differentiating oneself from an iconic predecessor (Warren Buffett), and how new leaders use early, decisive actions to establish credibility and organizational direction. These are well-studied phenomena in organizational psychology around CEO transitions and impression management.
