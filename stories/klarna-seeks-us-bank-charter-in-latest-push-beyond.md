## Summary
Klarna is seeking a U.S. bank charter as part of its strategic expansion beyond its core buy now, pay later business, joining other fintech and crypto companies pursuing traditional banking licenses.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Klarna's pursuit of a bank charter reflects a significant organizational identity transformation—shifting from a disruptive fintech challenger to a regulated banking institution, which requires fundamental changes in culture, risk tolerance, compliance mindset, and leadership capabilities.
