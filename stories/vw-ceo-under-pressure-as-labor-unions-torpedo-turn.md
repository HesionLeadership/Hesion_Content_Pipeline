# VW CEO Under Pressure as Labor Unions Torpedo Turnaround Plan

**Source:** Bloomberg  
**Published:** Sat, 11 Jul 2026 05:00:00 GMT

## Summary
Volkswagen CEO Oliver Blume is struggling to advance a major corporate turnaround plan after labor unions on the supervisory board blocked initial approval, highlighting deep internal conflict over the company's restructuring direction.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates the challenge of leading transformational change in organizations with strong co-determination structures, where executive authority is constrained by powerful internal stakeholders with competing interests, requiring coalition-building and stakeholder alignment before strategic execution.
