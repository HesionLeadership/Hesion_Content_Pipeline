## Summary
Chipotle's COO Jason Kidd takes employees to weekly dinners to identify future leaders, evaluating them on four key traits including being team players who 'own the outcome.'

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects informal talent identification through direct leader-employee interaction, bypassing traditional assessment pipelines. The emphasis on ownership and teamwork aligns with research on psychological ownership and organizational citizenship behavior as predictors of leadership emergence.

## Leadership Lesson
Senior leaders who invest personal time in observing employees in low-pressure, informal settings can identify leadership potential that formal performance reviews miss. CHROs should consider how structured-yet-informal talent spotting complements succession planning systems.
