# Bluesky’s interim CEO, Toni Schneider, drops the ‘interim’

**Source:** TechCrunch  
**Published:** Fri, 10 Jul 2026 21:51:55 +0000

## Summary
Bluesky's interim CEO Toni Schneider has officially dropped the 'interim' title, committing fully to leading the unconventional social media platform after previously serving as CEO of Automattic and partner at True Ventures.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
The transition from interim to permanent CEO reflects a critical leadership legitimacy moment—removing the 'interim' label signals organizational commitment and reduces uncertainty for employees, which research shows can improve team alignment, morale, and strategic execution during formative growth stages.
