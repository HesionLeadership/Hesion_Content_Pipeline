# ‘Moana’ Falters at the Box Office, Casting Doubt on Disney’s Formula

**Source:** NYT Business RSS  
**Published:** Sun, 12 Jul 2026 17:39:04 +0000  
**URL:** [https://www.nytimes.com/2026/07/12/business/media/moana-box-office-disney.html](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/07/12/business/media/moana-box-office-disney.html)

## Summary
Disney's live-action remake of Moana underperformed at the box office, earning roughly $43 million over the weekend—about 30% below expectations—raising questions about the sustainability of Disney's remake strategy.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This highlights the organizational risk of strategic over-reliance on a proven formula (live-action remakes of animated hits), where past success can create an innovation trap and groupthink around a narrowing creative pipeline.
