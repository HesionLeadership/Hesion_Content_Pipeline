# A Once-Great Copper Miner Faces a Reckoning Just as AI Power Demand Soars

**Source:** Bloomberg  
**Published:** Tue, 07 Jul 2026 10:00:26 GMT  
**URL:** [https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-07/codelco-confronts-debt-output-concerns-as-copper-demand-soars-on-ai](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-07/codelco-confronts-debt-output-concerns-as-copper-demand-soars-on-ai)

## Summary
Chile's state-owned copper miner Codelco is facing a crisis involving a fatal workplace accident, inflated production figures, and $25 billion in debt, just as global demand for copper surges due to AI infrastructure needs.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Codelco's struggles illustrate how organizational dysfunction—including data integrity failures (inflated production figures), safety culture breakdowns (fatal accident), and the governance challenges inherent in state-owned enterprises—can compound into existential crises. This is a textbook case of organizational decline where leadership accountability, transparency, and cultural rot intersect.
