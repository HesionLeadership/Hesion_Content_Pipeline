## Summary
The story discusses how AI and automated theorem proving may disrupt the academic mathematics community, where status and career advancement have been built around the production and verification of mathematical proofs—a 'theorem economy.'

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects how technological disruption can undermine established professional hierarchies and status systems within academic organizations, forcing a rethinking of what constitutes valued expertise and how career incentives are structured.
