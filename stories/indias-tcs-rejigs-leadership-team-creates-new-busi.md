## Summary
Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), India's largest IT services company, has restructured its leadership team and created new business units, signaling a significant organizational redesign effort.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Large-scale leadership restructuring and the creation of new business units reflect strategic organizational design choices that impact reporting structures, role clarity, and power dynamics. Such reorganizations can either energize or destabilize talent depending on how change is communicated and managed.

## Leadership Lesson
When restructuring leadership teams and creating new business units, CHROs and COOs should prioritize clear communication of rationale, ensure role clarity to prevent turf wars, and actively manage the transition period to maintain engagement and reduce uncertainty among both leaders and their teams.
