# In San Francisco, Even $180,000 Tech Salaries Are No Longer Enough

**Source:** NYT Most Popular  
**Published:** 2026-06-29  
**URL:** [https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/29/technology/san-francisco-tech-salaries.html](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/29/technology/san-francisco-tech-salaries.html)

## Summary
Tech workers in San Francisco earning six-figure salaries feel they can no longer compete financially with employees at AI companies like OpenAI and Anthropic, raising concerns about affordability and status within the tech ecosystem.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This story touches on relative deprivation theory and social comparison in workplaces—how intra-industry compensation disparities affect morale, retention, and perceived status among workers who are objectively well-compensated but feel disadvantaged relative to a new elite tier.
