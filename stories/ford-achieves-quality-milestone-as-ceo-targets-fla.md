# Ford achieves quality milestone, as CEO targets flawless new vehicle launches

## Summary
Ford CEO Jim Farley claims the automaker has reached a quality milestone and is targeting flawless new vehicle launches, learning from past quality and recall issues that hurt earnings and reputation.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects organizational learning and cultural transformation under CEO leadership — shifting from a reactive quality culture to a proactive one, which requires systemic changes in processes, accountability, and mindset across the organization.
