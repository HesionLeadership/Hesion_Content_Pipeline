An article exploring the concept of 'social physics' in conversation, examining how communication patterns and dynamics affect group interactions and outcomes.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Communication patterns within teams and organizations are a core topic in organizational psychology—research on turn-taking, information flow, and conversational dynamics directly relates to team effectiveness, psychological safety, and collective intelligence.
