# OPEC+ Emerges From War to Threat of Oil Surplus

## Summary
OPEC+ key members have agreed on another production hike despite emerging from geopolitical conflict, even as the market faces a potential oil surplus with little demand for additional barrels.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This highlights coalition decision-making dynamics and groupthink risks within OPEC+, where members may prioritize internal political cohesion and signaling unity over responding rationally to external market signals.
