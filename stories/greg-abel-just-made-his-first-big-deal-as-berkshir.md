## Summary
Greg Abel, newly installed as Berkshire Hathaway CEO succeeding Warren Buffett, has completed his first major acquisition by purchasing homebuilder Taylor Morrison at what appears to be a bargain valuation.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This story highlights the critical leadership succession dynamic—how a new CEO establishes credibility and continuity by making early strategic moves that align with the predecessor's values (value investing, bargain pricing), while also asserting independent decision-making authority.
