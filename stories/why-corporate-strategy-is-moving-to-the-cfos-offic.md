# Why corporate strategy is moving to the CFO’s office

**Source:** Fortune  
**Published:** Mon, 13 Jul 2026 10:29:54 +0000  
**URL:** [https://fortune.com/2026/07/13/why-corporate-strategy-moving-cfo-office/](https://fortune.com/2026/07/13/why-corporate-strategy-moving-cfo-office/)

## Summary
According to a McKinsey forum, the corporate strategy function is increasingly reporting to CFOs rather than CEOs or separate strategy officers, signaling a structural shift in how organizations house strategic decision-making.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This shift reflects a broader organizational trend toward financialization of strategy, raising questions about how reporting structures shape strategic thinking, risk tolerance, and whether finance-driven strategy prioritizes short-term metrics over long-term vision and innovation.

## Strength Score

The migration of strategy to the CFO's office has significant implications for organizational culture, decision-making frameworks, cross-functional influence, and how companies balance financial discipline with creative or transformational strategies.
