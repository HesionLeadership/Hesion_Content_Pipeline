## Summary
A longitudinal analysis spanning 60 years examines how attitudes about women in leadership have evolved, revealing persistent gender gaps in how men and women perceive evaluation standards, bias, and opportunity in organizations.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This directly addresses gender dynamics in leadership perception, highlighting how differential experiences of evaluation and opportunity shape organizational culture, talent retention, and leadership pipelines.
