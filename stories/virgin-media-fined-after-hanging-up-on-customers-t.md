# Virgin Media fined after hanging up on customers trying to cancel contracts

## Summary
Virgin Media was fined by the UK regulator after millions of customer phone calls were likely mishandled over nearly three years, with the company hanging up on customers attempting to cancel their contracts.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This reflects a systemic organizational culture issue where customer retention metrics likely incentivized employees to prevent cancellations through unethical means, highlighting how misaligned performance incentives and inadequate oversight can drive widespread dysfunctional behavior across an organization.
