# Hotel Workers Hoped the World Cup Would Boost Their Hours. It Didn’t.

**Source:** NYT Business RSS  
**Published:** Fri, 10 Jul 2026 09:02:25 +0000  
**URL:** [https://www.nytimes.com/2026/07/10/business/hotel-workers-world-cup.html](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/07/10/business/hotel-workers-world-cup.html)

## Summary
Hotel workers in World Cup host cities expected increased hours and income from the event, but hotel bookings have lagged behind expectations despite large match attendance, leaving workers struggling financially.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This highlights the gap between organizational promises/expectations and worker reality during major events, touching on workforce planning failures, the psychological contract between employers and hourly workers, and how event-driven economic assumptions can leave vulnerable workers bearing the cost of miscalculation.

## Strength Score
