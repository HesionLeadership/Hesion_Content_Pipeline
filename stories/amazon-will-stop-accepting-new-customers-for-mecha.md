# Amazon will stop accepting new customers for Mechanical Turk

**Source:** TechCrunch  
**Published:** Sun, 05 Jul 2026 17:43:36 +0000  
**URL:** [https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/05/amazon-will-stop-accepting-new-customers-for-mechanical-turk/](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/05/amazon-will-stop-accepting-new-customers-for-mechanical-turk/)

## Summary
Amazon is stopping new customer sign-ups for Mechanical Turk, signaling the potential end of its pioneering crowdsourced labor marketplace.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
The sunsetting of Mechanical Turk raises questions about how organizations manage the lifecycle of gig/crowdsourced labor platforms and the psychological contract with a distributed, precarious workforce that built its livelihood around the platform.
