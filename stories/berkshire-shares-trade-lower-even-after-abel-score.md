## Summary
Berkshire Hathaway shares traded lower despite Greg Abel receiving positive marks from the investment community for his command of the company's operations at the annual meeting, and strong earnings results.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This highlights the leadership succession challenge: even when a successor demonstrates competence and earns stakeholder confidence, markets may still discount the transition from an iconic founder-figure like Buffett, illustrating how organizational identity tied to a single leader creates valuation risk.
