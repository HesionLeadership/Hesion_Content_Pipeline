# Meta removes controversial AI feature on Instagram after backlash

**Source:** TechCrunch  
**Published:** Fri, 10 Jul 2026 23:55:07 +0000

## Summary
Meta removed a controversial AI feature from Instagram after receiving significant user backlash, acknowledging the feature 'missed the mark.'

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This illustrates organizational responsiveness to stakeholder feedback and the tension between innovation speed and user-centered design—a common challenge when product teams prioritize feature deployment over adequate user testing and consent frameworks.

## Original Story Summary
"Our intent was to provide a useful creative tool and to give people control over whether their public content could be referenced in this way," the company said in a blog post. "We've heard the feedback that this feature missed the mark, so it's no longer available."
