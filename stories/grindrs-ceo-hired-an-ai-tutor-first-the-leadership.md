## Summary
Grindr's CEO hired an AI tutor before rolling out AI tools company-wide, modeling the learning process himself to prepare employees for technological change rather than simply mandating adoption.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Leaders who personally model desired behaviors before asking others to change reduce resistance and build psychological safety around new initiatives. This reflects transformational leadership principles where leaders demonstrate vulnerability and learning orientation to legitimize the change process.

## Leadership Lesson
Before rolling out major organizational changes, leaders should visibly engage with the change themselves first. CHROs and COOs should design change management strategies where senior leaders publicly model the learning curve, normalizing struggle and reducing employee anxiety about new competency demands.
