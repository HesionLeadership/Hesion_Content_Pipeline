# Want Workers to Reskill? Show Them Who They Can Become.

## Summary
New research highlights that reskilling programs are more effective when they help workers envision a new professional identity and connect training to concrete employment outcomes.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This directly addresses identity-based motivation in workforce development—showing that aligning training design with workers' sense of possible future selves significantly enhances engagement, learning transfer, and career transitions.
