## Summary
ITV is selling its media and entertainment division to Sky for £1.6 billion, with both companies framing the deal as creating a stronger competitor to global streaming giants.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
This major divestiture raises significant questions about organizational identity and integration challenges—how Sky absorbs ITV's creative talent and culture, and how ITV redefines its purpose and structure after shedding a core division.
