# Wayve launches $85M employee tender offer at $8.5B valuation

**Source:** TechCrunch

## Summary
Wayve, an autonomous driving AI startup, is launching an $85 million employee tender offer at an $8.5 billion valuation, part of a broader trend among AI startups using such offers to attract and retain talent.

## Organizational Psychology Angle
Employee tender offers represent a powerful retention and motivation tool in competitive talent markets, providing liquidity to employees holding illiquid equity—directly addressing psychological needs for financial security and perceived fairness of compensation, which can reduce turnover intention in high-demand fields like AI.
