## Summary
Independent studio Neon acquired a documentary about OpenAI and Sam Altman after Amazon dropped the film, reportedly due to Amazon's financial investment in OpenAI creating a conflict of interest.

This story illustrates how corporate financial entanglements can compromise editorial independence and decision-making autonomy—Amazon's investment in OpenAI effectively vetoed its own content division's creative choices, revealing tensions between business strategy and organizational integrity.
